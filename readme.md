# 🚀 YouthX Research
**Empowering the Next Generation of AI Architects & Open-Source Researchers.**

YouthX Research is an independent, youth-led research collective dedicated to exploring the frontiers of **Artificial Intelligence**, **Deep Learning**, and **Distributed Systems**. We bridge the gap between academic theory and production-grade engineering.

---

## 🧬 Our Research Pillars

### 🤖 Generative AI & LLMs
We build and fine-tune Large Language Models from the ground up. From understanding the mathematical foundations of **Transformers** to optimizing **inference pipelines** for edge devices.
* *Key Project:* `custom-gpt-trainer` - A minimalist, high-performance GPT implementation.

### 👁️ Computer Vision
Exploring the intersection of CNNs and real-world applications. We develop models that see, understand, and categorize the world with precision.
* *Key Project:* `AnimalVision` - PyTorch-based image classification suite.

### 🛠️ Open Source Tooling
Creating the "glue" that makes AI research accessible. We develop automation scripts, data processing pipelines, and deployment tools for researchers worldwide.
